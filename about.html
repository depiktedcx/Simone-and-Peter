Long Bay

Long Bay is a suburb of the North Shore, part of the Auckland metropolitan area in northern New Zealand. The coastline is occupied by the Long Bay Regional Park, and the shoreline is in Long Bay-Okura Marine Reserve, which opened in 1995. The beach is sandy and swimming is safe.
